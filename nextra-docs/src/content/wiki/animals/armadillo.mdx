# Armadillo

The armadillo is a shy, armor-plated creature that prefers to avoid confrontation. When threatened, it rolls into a tight defensive ball to protect itself!

## Quick Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Health** | 6 hearts |
| **Drops** | Armadillo Scute (when brushed) |
| **Defense** | Curls into protective ball |
| **Habitat** | Savanna, Badlands |

## Behavior

### Defensive Curling

Armadillos have a unique defense mechanism - when they sense danger, they curl into a tight ball! This protective posture makes them nearly invulnerable while curled up.

**Try this:** Approach an armadillo too quickly and watch it roll into a ball. If you back away and wait patiently, it will eventually uncurl and resume its normal activities.

**Tip:** Move slowly and crouch when approaching armadillos to avoid startling them. They're much more likely to stay uncurled if you give them space!

### Natural Shyness

These cautious creatures are always on the lookout for potential threats. They're particularly skittish around sudden movements and loud noises.

**Did you know?** Baby armadillos are even more cautious than adults! They'll curl up at the slightest hint of danger, making them extra challenging to interact with.

## Finding Armadillos

Armadillos make their home in warm, dry climates. You'll find them wandering through:
- Savanna biomes
- Badlands biomes

They prefer open areas where they can easily spot approaching predators.

## Obtaining Scutes

Armadillos are the primary source of armadillo scutes, which are essential for crafting wolf armor!

### Brushing Method

The best way to obtain scutes is by brushing armadillos with a brush. This gentle approach doesn't harm the creature and yields scutes over time.

**How to brush:**
1. Craft a brush (stick + feather + copper ingot)
2. Slowly approach an armadillo while crouching
3. Wait for it to uncurl if it has rolled up
4. Use the brush on the armadillo
5. Collect the scute that drops

**Tip:** Patience is key! Rushing the process will only cause the armadillo to curl up repeatedly. Take your time and let the armadillo feel safe.

### Natural Drops

Armadillos occasionally shed scutes naturally as they grow, though this is less reliable than brushing.

## Interaction Tips

**Building Trust**
- Approach slowly and crouch to appear less threatening
- Avoid sudden movements or jumping near them
- Give them space when they curl up - they'll uncurl when they feel safe
- Create a calm environment by keeping other mobs away

**Working with Baby Armadillos**
Baby armadillos are adorable but extremely cautious. They require even more patience than adults:
- They curl up more frequently
- They take longer to uncurl
- They're more sensitive to nearby threats
- Extra care is needed when attempting to brush them

## Wolf Armor Connection

The scutes you collect from armadillos serve an important purpose: crafting wolf armor to protect your loyal companions!

**Try this:** Once you've collected enough scutes, craft wolf armor to keep your tamed wolves safe during adventures. Your armadillo friends indirectly help protect your wolf pack!

## Conservation Notes

Armadillos are peaceful creatures that contribute to the ecosystem. While they don't directly interact with other animals in complex ways, their cautious nature makes them an interesting study in defensive behaviors.

**Did you know?** An armadillo's shell is strong enough to deflect most attacks while curled. This remarkable defense mechanism has helped them survive in harsh biomes for ages!

## Summary

Armadillos are gentle, defensive creatures that reward patient players with valuable scutes. Their unique curling behavior makes every interaction a lesson in patience and observation. Whether you're collecting scutes for wolf armor or simply enjoying their cautious nature, armadillos add a charming dynamic to the savanna and badlands biomes.

Remember: slow and steady wins the race when working with these shy creatures!
