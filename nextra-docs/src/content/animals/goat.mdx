## Behaviors

### Ramming

Goats charge and ram threats and other entities.

- Long-distance charge attack
- High knockback
- Cooldown between rams
- Can accidentally ram trees

### Mountain Movement

Goats are adapted for mountain terrain.

- Reduced fall damage
- High jump ability
- Sure-footed on slopes
- Preference for elevated terrain

### Grazing

Goats eat various vegetation.

- Less selective than other grazers
- Can eat tougher plants
- Reduced hunger from diverse diet

### Herd Behavior

Goats form loose herds.

- Less cohesive than sheep
- Follow each other over terrain
- Alert calls when threatened

## Scientific Basis

Based on mountain goat behavior including their climbing abilities and territorial ramming.
