## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Health | 15-30 HP (varies) |
| Social Type | Herd/Caravan |
| Tameable | Yes (rideable: No) |
| Special Ability | Spit attack |

## Behaviors

### Caravan Following

Llamas form caravans when led.

- Follow other llamas on leads
- Up to 10 llamas per caravan
- Maintain spacing in line

### Spit Defense

Llamas spit at threats and wolves.

- Ranged attack
- Causes knockback
- Specifically targets wolves
- Cooldown between spits

### Pack Animal

Llamas can carry items.

- Chest storage when equipped
- Storage size varies by strength
- Carry items in caravans

### Herd Behavior

- Stay near other llamas
- Follow trader llamas
- Alert responses to threats

## Trader Llamas

Trader llamas have additional behaviors:

- Follow wandering trader
- Enhanced defensive behavior
- Despawn with trader

## Scientific Basis

Based on llama caravan behavior and their natural spitting defense mechanism.
