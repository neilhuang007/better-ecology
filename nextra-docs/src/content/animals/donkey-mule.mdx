## Donkey

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Health | 15-30 HP |
| Tameable | Yes |
| Rideable | Yes |
| Chest Storage | Yes |

### Unique Behaviors

**Pack Animal:**
- Can equip chest
- Storage capacity
- Carry items while ridden

**Breeding:**
- Breed donkeys with golden items
- Cross with horse to produce mule

## Mule

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Health | 15-30 HP |
| Tameable | Yes |
| Rideable | Yes |
| Chest Storage | Yes |
| Breeding | Cannot breed |

### Unique Behaviors

**Sterility:**
- Cannot produce offspring
- Hybrid of horse and donkey

**Enhanced Carrying:**
- Same chest storage as donkey
- Often stronger than donkeys

## Shared Behaviors

Both share horse-family behaviors:

- Kick defense
- Rearing
- Herd dynamics
- Social grooming

See [Horse](/animals/horse) for full behavior details.

## Comparison

| Feature | Horse | Donkey | Mule |
|---------|-------|--------|------|
| Speed | Fast | Medium | Medium |
| Jump | High | Low | Medium |
| Storage | No | Yes | Yes |
| Breeding | Yes | Yes | No |

## Scientific Basis

Based on real equine behavior and the biological characteristics of donkeys and mules.
