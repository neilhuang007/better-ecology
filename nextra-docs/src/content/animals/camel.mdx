## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Health | 32 HP (16 hearts) |
| Habitat | Desert |
| Riders | 2 players |
| Special Ability | Dash |

## Behaviors

### Dash

Camels can perform a charging dash.

- High-speed burst movement
- Cooldown between dashes
- Crosses gaps
- Recharge time visible

### Sitting

Camels naturally sit down.

- Sit when idle
- Stand when interacted
- Rest position for players to mount
- Reduced hitbox when sitting

### Two-Rider Capacity

Camels carry two players.

- Front and back positions
- Front rider controls
- Both riders can use items

### Desert Adaptation

Camels are suited for desert environments.

- No speed penalty on sand
- Heat tolerance (no overheating)
- Can cross cactus safely (tall enough)

### Herd Behavior

Camels form loose groups.

- Follow other camels
- Shared resting behavior
- Social sitting

## Scientific Basis

Based on real camel adaptations to desert environments and their historical use as pack animals.
